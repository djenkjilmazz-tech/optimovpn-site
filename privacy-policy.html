Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

1 Introduction

Welcome to OptimoVPN. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how OptimoVPN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our iOS application (the "App").

OptimoVPN is a Virtual Private Network (VPN) service. The very purpose of a VPN is to protect your online privacy — and we take that responsibility seriously. We have designed this service with a strict privacy-first approach.

Core Privacy Commitment: We do NOT log, monitor, inspect, or store any VPN tunnel traffic, browsing history, connection logs, IP addresses assigned to you during a VPN session, or DNS queries made through our VPN. Your online activity is private.

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide and improve our service. We categorize what we collect as follows:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • No account required for the free tier. You can use OptimoVPN without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to OptimoVPN Premium, your purchase is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive your payment card number or billing address. We receive a subscription status confirmation from Apple (via RevenueCat) to determine if your account is active.
  • Support communications: If you contact us by email for support, we receive your email address and the contents of your message to respond to you.

2.2 Usage and Technical Data

We may collect limited technical and usage data to operate and improve the App. This data does NOT include your VPN traffic content.

  • Crash reports and diagnostics: Anonymous crash logs and error data to identify and fix bugs. These do not contain any VPN traffic or browsing history.
  • App performance data: General metrics such as app launch frequency and feature usage patterns, collected in an aggregated and anonymized form.
  • Subscription status: Whether you are on the free or premium tier, to show the correct features and ads.

What We Do NOT Collect: We do not collect, log, or retain your VPN tunnel traffic, DNS queries, browsing history, originating IP address during VPN sessions, connection timestamps tied to your identity, or the destination of any network requests made through the VPN.

2.3 Advertising Data (Free Tier Only)

If you use the free, ad-supported tier of OptimoVPN, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect certain device and advertising identifiers as described in Section 3 below. Premium subscribers do not see ads, and this data collection does not apply to them.

3 Third-Party Services

OptimoVPN integrates with the following third-party services to deliver our features. Each third party operates under its own privacy policy.

3.1 Google AdMob (Free Tier Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or contextual ads. This includes:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • Device type, OS version, and general location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (views, taps)

App Tracking Transparency (ATT): In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, we will ask for your permission before allowing AdMob to access your IDFA for personalized advertising. You can change this permission at any time in your iOS Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you decline, you may still see ads, but they will be less personalized. Premium subscribers are not shown ads and are not subject to this data collection.

Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and validate purchase receipts. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous device identifier
  • Purchase and subscription receipts from Apple
  • Subscription status and entitlement information

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment information. Their privacy policy is at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.3 Apple (App Store & Payments)

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store infrastructure. Apple handles your payment information and we never have access to your credit card details. Apple's privacy policy is at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3.4 VPN Gate (Free Server Infrastructure)

For free-tier VPN connectivity, OptimoVPN uses publicly available VPN servers from the VPN Gate Project, a public VPN relay service operated by the University of Tsukuba, Japan. When you connect through a VPN Gate server:

  • Your connection is routed through a volunteer-operated relay server.
  • The VPN Gate project may maintain connection logs on their servers in accordance with their own logging policy.
  • We recommend reviewing the VPN Gate privacy policy at vpngate.net if you have concerns about their logging practices.
  • Premium subscribers connect through our curated server infrastructure and are not affected by VPN Gate's policies.

4 How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To establish your VPN connection, authenticate your subscription status, and provide the app's core functionality.
  • Service improvement: To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve app performance using anonymized diagnostic data.
  • Advertising (free tier only): To display advertisements to free users, supporting the free tier of the service. This is handled by Google AdMob as described above.
  • Subscription management: To verify whether your Premium subscription is active and grant the appropriate level of access.
  • Customer support: To respond to your queries and resolve issues when you contact us.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations, and to enforce our Terms of Use.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising beyond what AdMob uses for its own ad targeting on the free tier.

5 Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • Encryption in transit: All communication between the App and our servers uses TLS encryption. VPN traffic is encrypted using AES-256, the same standard used by governments and financial institutions.
  • Minimal data retention: Because we collect minimal data, there is minimal data to secure. We retain support email communications only as long as necessary to resolve your issue, and anonymized diagnostic data is retained for up to 12 months.
  • No traffic logs: Since we do not log VPN traffic, there is no traffic log data that could be breached or disclosed.
  • Third-party security: We require our third-party service providers (RevenueCat, AdMob) to maintain appropriate security standards.

Despite our best efforts, no security system is impenetrable. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. We respect these rights and will respond to valid requests within the timeframes required by applicable law.

6.1 Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA Residents)

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may ask us to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure ("Right to be forgotten"): You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to restrict processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing of your data based on our legitimate interests.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6.2 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

  • Right to know: You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell personal information. If this changes, you will have the right to opt-out of the sale.
  • Right to non-discrimination: You will not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your privacy rights.

6.3 Advertising Opt-Out

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by:

  • Declining the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt when first presented in the App.
  • Going to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and disabling tracking for OptimoVPN.
  • Upgrading to OptimoVPN Premium, which is completely ad-free.

To exercise any of your rights, please contact us at the email address in Section 9.

7 Children's Privacy

OptimoVPN is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the address in Section 9 and we will take steps to delete such information.

If you are between 13 and 17 years of age, you must have the consent of a parent or guardian before using OptimoVPN.
